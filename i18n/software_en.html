Libraries Used

The following libraries play a major role for Rocksi's functionality, but there are dozens of secundary dependencies. For a full list see the source code at https://github.com/ndahn/Rocksi.

  • Blockly
  • JS-Interpreter
  • three.js
  • cannon-es
  • urdf-loaders
  • xacro-parser
  • Font Awesome
  • UIL
  • split.js
  • lozad

Robot Models

The 3D models used in Rocksi are provided by the robots' manufacturers as part of their ROS interfaces.

  • Franka (Franka Emika)
  • Niryo NED (Niryo)
  • Sawyer (Rethink Robotics)
  • Robotiq Grippers
